üöÄ Getting Started

How do I create an account? ‚ñº
Creating an account is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. You can also sign up directly via team invitation links.
What assessments are available? ‚ñº
PsychSync offers multiple assessment frameworks including MBTI, Big Five, Enneagram, Predictive Index, StrengthsFinder, DISC, and Social Styles. Each provides unique insights into personality and work preferences.
How long does an assessment take? ‚ñº
Most assessments take between 10-30 minutes depending on the framework. MBTI and Big Five typically take 15-20 minutes, while StrengthsFinder may take up to 30 minutes. You can save progress and continue later.
Can I take assessments on my phone? ‚ñº
Yes! PsychSync is fully mobile-responsive. You can complete assessments on any device - desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Your progress is automatically saved across devices.

üìã Assessments

Are my assessment results private? ‚ñº
Yes! Your individual responses are confidential. Only aggregated team-level insights are shared with your organization admin. You control whether to share your personal results with team members.
Can I retake an assessment? ‚ñº
Yes, you can retake assessments. We recommend waiting at least 6 months between retakes for the most accurate results, as personality traits are relatively stable but can evolve over time.
What if I don't understand my results? ‚ñº
Each result comes with detailed explanations. We also provide video guides and written resources. For Professional and Enterprise plans, you can schedule a consultation with our team psychology experts.
How accurate are the assessments? ‚ñº
Our assessments use scientifically validated frameworks with reliability scores of 0.7-0.9. Accuracy depends on honest responses. Results should be viewed as insights for self-discovery, not absolute truths.

üë• Teams & Collaboration

How do I invite team members? ‚ñº
Go to your team dashboard, click "Invite Members," and enter email addresses. Members receive an invitation link to join your team and complete assessments.
What are team compatibility scores? ‚ñº
Compatibility scores (0-100) indicate how well team members' work styles and personalities align. Higher scores suggest easier collaboration, while lower scores identify areas needing communication strategies.
Can I have multiple teams? ‚ñº
Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans support multiple teams within one organization. Each team can have different members and separate analytics.
